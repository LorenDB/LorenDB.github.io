Harmonica

I play the harmonica (albeit poorly). I use a Hohner Special 20 in the key of C for most of my playing, although I also have a Suzuki Chromatix SZX-48, an older Marine Band 365, and an older Blues Harp in G. My abilities currently extend only to some basic playing and some bending.
I heartily recommend the harmonica to anybody who wants to learn an instrument. There are numerous benefits of the the harmonica, including:

  • Simple portability - fits in your pocket
  • Versatile - plays many different genres
  • Helps expand lung capacity
  • Can be played with other instruments - just use a neck rack
  • ...and many more!
If you do end up learning harmonica due to this website, please drop me a line and let me know.
